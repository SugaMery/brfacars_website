What You Need to Rent a Car

To rent a car at Casablanca Airport, you will need:

  • A valid driver's license (an International Driving Permit may be required for non-Moroccan residents).
  • A credit card for the security deposit.
  • Your passport or ID for verification purposes.

Top Attractions to Visit with Your Rental Car

With your rental car, you can easily visit the top attractions in and around Casablanca:

  • Hassan II Mosque: One of the largest mosques in the world, featuring beautiful architecture and stunning views of the ocean.
  • La Corniche: A beautiful beachfront area perfect for a relaxing day out by the sea.
  • Royal Palace of Casablanca: Visit the impressive residence of the King of Morocco, located in the heart of the city.
  • Rabat: Take a short drive to the capital city of Morocco and explore historical landmarks like the Kasbah of the Udayas and the Mausoleum of Mohammed V.
